Odin Recipes

Lasagna

This is the classic lasagne alla Bolognese recipe from the Emilia
in Northern Italy. The Bolognese sauce is made with a mixture of beef and pork mince.
The addition of prosciutto, red wine, cinnamon, and nutmeg make it
truly authentic.

Meatloaf

Meatloaf is a dish of ground meat that has been combined with other
and formed into the shape of a loaf, then baked or smoked. The final shape is
either hand-formed on a baking tray, or pan-formed by cooking it in a loaf pan.

Chicken Teriyaki

Teri (照り) means “luster” given by the sweet soy sauce marinade,
while yaki (焼き) has a wider definition which can denote “cook or pan-fry or grill”.
When translated literally, the dish means “shiny or glossy grilled chicken” as it describes
the lustrous glaze on the chicken created by the teriyaki sauce.
